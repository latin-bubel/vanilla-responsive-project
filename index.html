About

Ariana

Ariana Grande is a multi-talented artist and a prominent figure in the world of entertainment. With her powerful voice and undeniable charisma, she has captivated audiences worldwide. Rising to fame through her early acting roles in Nickelodeon's "Victorious" and "Sam & Cat," Ariana transitioned seamlessly into a successful music career.

Her distinctive vocal range, characterized by its impressive whistle tones and soulful delivery, has earned her numerous accolades and a dedicated fan base. Beyond her music, Grande is known for her philanthropic efforts, advocating for important causes and using her platform to make a positive impact on society. With a unique sense of style and an unwavering dedication to her craft, Ariana Grande continues to be a pop culture icon and an inspiration to many.

Ariana Grande at the BBC

Ariana Grande at the BBC is a BBC television special featuring Ariana Grande. Grande performed thirteen songs during the hour-long special, and was interviewed by Davina McCall. Grande performed 9 songs, backed by an all-female orchestra.

Date

Recorded on 7 September and aired on 1 November 2018.

Setlist

No Tears Left To Cry, Dangerous Woman, Breathin, Goodnight N Go, R.E.M, Only 1, God Is A Woman, Love Me Harder / One Last Time.

Amazon Prime Day

Vevo's Official Live Performance

In 2021, Ariana Grande collaborated with Vevo for a live performance series on her YouTube channel, filmed in Los Angeles, California. This followed their previous collaboration on the Vevo Presents series, featuring live renditions of songs from Grande's 2016 album, "Dangerous Woman."

R.E.M Beauty

R.E.M. Beauty, Ariana Grande's makeup brand, has a clear mission: to cater to people of all backgrounds. Grande herself expressed her passion for making everyone feel incredible with her products, emphasizing the personal nature of makeup.

The brand believeas that makeup is a powerful means of self-expression, and their mission is to provide clients with the means to amplify their inner beauty and creativity.

"i love make up, i love how it makes me feel, i love how instrumental it is to my art and to one's sense of self and artistic expression. i want to help encourage others to feel even more beautiful, to create, to tell their stories creatively and express themselves" -ari.
